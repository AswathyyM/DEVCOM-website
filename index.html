About

Devcom CEC is a student-led initiative that encourages creativity and leadership through weekly sessions, workshops, and real-world projects. With a focus on software development, design, and more, we foster a sustainable, collaborative environment for growth and learning.

Events

Hall of Code

The Hall of Code was the inaugural event for Devcom, centered around hands-on coding challenges and collaborative projects. It fostered teamwork and problem-solving skills, allowing participants to connect, share ideas, and gain practical experience. This successful event laid the groundwork for future structured sessions and workshops.

Weekly Meetings

Devcom's Weekly Meetings have transitioned from biweekly sessions to create a consistent learning environment. Each meeting focuses on a specific topic, enabling participants to explore new technologies and concepts while collaborating on projects. These gatherings strengthen community ties and enhance the overall learning experience.

Stay updated with our upcoming events, including hackathons, workshops, coding challenges, and speaker sessions designed to enhance your technical skills and foster collaboration. Keep an eye on this space for exciting opportunities to learn and grow!
